About Me

Growing up as a child, there were not many kids in my neighborhood. To the dissappointment of my parents disappointment, I didn’t make friends as a kid, favoring videogames instead. I liked playing videogames because I didn’t need other people to have a good time. I could be independent, something that defined me as a child.

Something that I would like to do when I graduate, and maybe after college, is to live in another country for a year. Many other places have such rich cultures, which I would like to really dive into. I would like to learn the language, try all the food, get a job, and really get to know the people who live there. I feel that an experience like this would be helpful in understanding the ideas and lifestyles of others.
